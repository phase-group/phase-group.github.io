Welcome to the PHASE Group

The PHASE Group investigates nonlinear and Hamiltonian dynamics in astrophysical systems, focusing on orbital structure, phase-space evolution, stability, and chaos.

Our work spans galactic dynamics, compact objects, and relativistic systems, combining analytical theory with numerical simulations and modern chaos diagnostics.

Research Highlights

Nonlinear Dynamics

Phase-space structure, chaos indicators, and long-term orbital stability.

Galactic Cores

Orbital motion and instability near dense galactic nuclei and halo potentials.

Compact Objects

Dynamics around black holes and relativistic systems with spin and multipole structure.

Our Approach

We emphasize physically motivated models, rigorous dynamical-systems analysis, and reproducible numerical experiments. Our goal is to uncover the fundamental mechanisms driving complexity in gravitational systems.
