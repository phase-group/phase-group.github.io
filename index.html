Introduction

The PHASE Group studies nonlinear and Hamiltonian dynamics in astrophysical gravitational systems, with emphasis on phase-space structure, orbital stability, chaos, and time variability.

Our research spans galactic dynamics, compact objects, and relativistic systems, combining analytical theory, numerical simulations, and observational X-ray data analysis.

A major component of our work involves the study and modeling of quasi-periodic oscillations (QPOs), linking observed variability to nonlinear dynamical mechanisms and relativistic motion in strong-gravity environments.

Current Research Topics

  • Nonlinear dynamics and chaos
  • Phase-space structure and stability
  • Galactic core and halo dynamics
  • Compact objects and relativistic motion
  • X-ray variability and QPO modeling
  • Chaos indicators (SALI, mLCE, basin entropy)
